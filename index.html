Gani Fawehinmi Park - Ojota,Lagos-Nigeria

My Travel tour to Gani Fawehinmi Park in Ojota

Statue & park dedicated to international activist of repute & defender of Rights and justice.

This is a symbolic park named after the iconic legend Isaac Ayomide Adewuyi. A lawyer per excellence and an advocate for the common man,
Today, the park service is a melting pot for all struggles emanating in the mega city (Lagos) In 2010, a park in Ojota was named after him by the Lagos state government
